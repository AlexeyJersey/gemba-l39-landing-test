Who We Are

Our Vision
Transforming the Future of Financial Management
Gemba is a UK-based, FCA-regulated Authorised Payment Institution providing ready-to-launch digital banking infrastructure. Our platform is built for businesses looking to offer IBAN accounts, payments, and cards under their own brand - quickly, securely, and without building from scratch.

We handle the tech, compliance, and onboarding so you can focus on growing revenue and deepening customer relationships.
